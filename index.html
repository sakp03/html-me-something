Monte Ne Inn

An area that once started out as a resort has left behind this quaint little restaurant located in Rogers, AR. With its friendly faces and homestyle tradition where everyone passes the plate, Monte Ne Inn has become a place synonymous with great "fried chicken". Come try this place out with its cozy atmosphere, seasoned chicken, and other fixins. You'll fall in love with their freshly baked bread and mouth watering apple butter. And if the rest hasn't already gotten your attention, who knew that bean soup could be so good. Don't miss out on this hidden gem.

Juicy Tails

Whether you are craving crab legs, crawfish, shrimp, etc. Juicy Tails is your go to place for freshly steamed seafood. This place is not for the neat freaks in the world. You will get your hands dirty, but the reward is so worth the effort. With locations in Fayetteville and Rogers, this hidden gem is a must for seafood lovers.

Thai Taste

When you are always in the hunt for some good authentic cooking, hidden gems like Thai taste are a treat. Reminiscent of Lao cooking, this place has me going back frequently. From curries to papaya salad, this place does not disappoint the taste buds. Located a bit off the beaten path of Springdale, AR.

Sushi House

If your are looking for Japanese food, don't pass by Sushi House. This gem is located off of 14th street in Bentonville, AR. From their Sams roll to their udon, you will not regret this choice. This place is always packed during lunch. Looking for a relax atmosphere where you can spend hours, this is NOT the place for you. They make the food fast and delicious, but don't try to stay too long in courtesy of other customers.

Chicot Hibachi

Lets face it, college students are always looking for more bang for their buck. Located off of MLK in Fayetteville, AR, Chicot is a great find for large portions. Decent price, good food, and large portions. How can one pass this up?

Ramen Nara

If you are a fan of ramen, then don't let this hidden gem pass you by. Their Tonkotsu ramen is creamy, savory and full of umami flavors. And if you're not looking for ramen, give their chicken karaage (fried chicken with a gluten-free soy-sesame marinade) a try. Located in Bentonville, AR.

Flying Fish

If you haven't enough seafood, give this place a try. Flying Fish is located off the square in Bentonville, AR. Their fried baskets are a must. However, if you are looking for a healthier option, they have a great grilled section ranging from shrimp to trout.

Pineapple Bliss

Sadly, this hidden gem is closed for the winter season. Located in the parking lot of Harps, off of exit 78 in Lowell, AR, Pineapple Bliss serves amazing softserve. You can go for the traditional must have pineapple or you can try their flavor of the week. With an always changing flavor selection, you could try cherry one week, orange the next, or even matcha based on the mood of the makers. With a variety of toppings, you can always find something new.

Fruitealicious

What started in Dallas has made its way to 14th and Main in Bentonville,AR. Whether you are craving creamy & milky or vibrant & fruity, Fruitealicious has a tea for you. Allowing the choice for sweetness serves all levels of sweet tooths. Whether you want to add boba, flan, popping bobas, or even jellies, they have a variety that can match your tastes. Don't let this hidden gem pass you by.
